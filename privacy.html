Privacy Policy

At DairiX, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our autonomous vending services.

Data Collection

  • Transactional Data: We collect details of purchases made via our rovers, including payment timestamps and product selections.
  • Location Data: Anonymous aggregate data on deployment zone demand to optimize rover routes.
  • Digital Wallet Info: We use secure, encrypted processing for GCash, Maya, and card payments. We do not store full payment credentials on our local hardware.

Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively to provide and improve our dairy distribution services. We do not sell user data to third-party marketing firms.
